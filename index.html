Basic Instructions

  1. Create a Wallet at https://www.myetherwallet.com
  2. Send Ethereum to your MyEtherWallet
  3. Send Ethereum to one of the Vending Machine Addresses
  4. Click “View your transaction”. If the vending machine was not empty, you will see a line that says ERC20 token. These are your cards!

How to see your cards

(in MyEtherWallet) click “Add Custom Token” for each of the current token
